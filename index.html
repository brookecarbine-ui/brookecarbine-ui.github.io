Petal and Chain

Petal and Chain began in my basement where a simple idea bloomed into something beautiful. Surrounded by years of collected beads, pendants, and forgotten treasures, I found joy in giving them new life.

About Us

We are a custom jewelry company that enjoys tansforming each piece into something timeless, meaningful, and full of character. What started out as a creative spark has grown into a boutique jewelry brand built on renewal, craftmanship and a lot of heart. At Petal and Chain, every piece tells a story. A story of reinvention, beauty, and the lasting connection between the past and whats yet to come.

Products

At Petal & Chain, we offer a beautiful variety of handmade jewelry, crafts, décor, and gifts—each piece designed to bring creativity and warmth into everyday life. Our jewelry line features thoughtfully handcrafted necklaces, earrings, and bracelets made with care and intention, blending elegance with a touch of boho charm. Beyond jewelry, we create unique handmade crafts, home décor pieces, and custom gift baskets that make perfect presents for any occasion, from birthdays and holidays to heartfelt thank-yous. Every product from Petal & Chain is made with love, inspired by nature and artistry, and crafted to spread beauty, joy, and a personal touch that you can feel in every detail
